About Me

Hobbyist photographer based in Seattle

Hi I'm Timothy! I am a 19-year-old Vietnamese American photographer based in Greater Seattle.

I started street photography amidst the pandemic as a reason to explore the city.

Armed with nothing more than my camera, I roam the streets of Seattle looking for moments to capture.


Currently I am a computer science student, as such this website was developed as a project to combine

my love for photography alongside my coding/web development abilities.


Thank you for learning more about my shared passion!
